# Concepts
### How Conservation of Energy Leads to Kirchhoff’s Rules
***If a single charge makes a complete loop around the circuit, conservation of energy says***: $$\Delta U_E =0$$
- In circuit terms $$\varSigma \Delta V =0$$
- A complete loop around a circuit has a potential difference of ZERO
### Kirchhoff’s Loop Rules without Junctions
***Steps to write Kirchhoff’s Loops***
1. Choose a starting point. The loop must start and end at the same point
2. Sketch in currents. Direction does not matter
3. Sketch in loops. Direction does not matter
4. Follow your loop. 
**Rules:**

| Battery                                      |        | Resistor                                   |        |
| -------------------------------------------- | ------ | ------------------------------------------ | ------ |
| Loop goes from negative to positive terminal | **+V** | Loop and current are in the same direction | **-V** |
| Loop goes from positive to negative terminal | **-V** | Loop and current are in opposite direction | **+V** |

### Kirchhoff’s Loop Rule
$$\varSigma \Delta V = +V \tiny battery \normalsize -V \tiny resistor = 0$$
$$V \tiny battery \normalsize = V \tiny resistor$$
**NOTE**: $$\varSigma \Delta V = 0$$
#### Kirchhoff’s Junction Rule
$$\varSigma I \tiny in \normalsize = \varSigma I \tiny out$$
