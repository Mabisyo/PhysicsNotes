--Projectile motion key notes: 

v0 = initial velocities 

---NOTE: No HORIZONTAL acceleration in PM

-Time a projectile is in the air (OF A CLIFF):

t = sqrt(2h/g)

-Projectile launched at an angle:

Time at max height-
t = (v0sin(theta))/g

Maximum height:
y = ((vo)^2(sintheta))/(2g)

Total time in air:
t = (2vosin(theta))/g

Range:
R = ((vo)^2(sin(2theta)))/g
